This section contains a wealth of information, related to Techkriti and its events. If you cannot find an answer to your question, make sure to contact us.

General questions

When is the Registration for XYZ event opening?

Most of the events are already open for registration. Some of the events have a limited registration window, you can take a look at it in the dashboard of the portal. For more updates, follow us on Facebook, twitter.

Can I submit my own Bootstrap templates or themes?

A lot of the content of the site has been submitted by the community. Whether it is a commercial element/template/theme or a free one, you are encouraged to contribute. All credits are published along with the resources.
Registration

How can I register ?

You can either use facebook login to register or any email address to register.

Do I need to pay before registration?

No, payment portal will be opened later to those who have registered.For more updates, follow us on Facebook, twitter.

I didn't get my activation mail on registeration. What should I do?

Just try to login again with your credentials. You will be resent the Activation Email.
