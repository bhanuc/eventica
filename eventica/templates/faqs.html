Registration

How can I register ?

You can either use facebook login to register or any email address to register.

Do I need to pay before registration?

No, payment portal will be opened later to those who have registered.For more updates, follow us on Facebook, twitter.

I didn't get my activation mail on registeration. What should I do?

Just try to login again with your credentials. You will be resent the Activation Email.

I was suddenly logged out of my account. Whats Happening?

It might happen very rarely. When a major change is being uploaded to the server.Sorry for the inconvenience caused.Please login again and continue from where you left.
Events

I am getting "one of the user is not registered" when I try to register my team ?

Your whole team needs to registered before you actually register and then you only need to enter the TechIDs of the members in different boxes. Entering anything other than a valid techid in the input-box would give you an error.If you are sure that you are following everything, then please leave a mail detailing everything to bhanuc@iitk.ac.in.
